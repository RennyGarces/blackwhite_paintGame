Paint game black and white

Instruccions:

  1. Press the shift button and change to white color whereas use the mouse.
  2. Press the Ctrl button whereas use the mouse to increase the black brushtroke.
  3. Press the Alt button whereas use the mouse to decrease the black brushtroke.
